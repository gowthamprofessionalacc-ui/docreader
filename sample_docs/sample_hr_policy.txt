Grahvity AI Systems — Employee Handbook (Abridged)
Last Updated: February 2025

=== Work Schedule & Remote Policy ===

Grahvity operates on a hybrid work model:
- Core hours: 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM IST (mandatory availability)
- Flexible hours: Employees may start as early as 8:00 AM or end as late as 8:00 PM
- Remote work: 3 days remote, 2 days in-office per week (Tuesday and Thursday are in-office days)
- Office location: WeWork Galaxy, Residency Road, Bangalore 560025

=== Leave Policy ===

Annual leave entitlement per calendar year:
- Casual Leave: 12 days
- Sick Leave: 8 days (medical certificate required for 3+ consecutive days)
- Earned Leave: 15 days (accrued monthly, encashable up to 10 days at year-end)
- Public Holidays: 12 days (as per Karnataka state calendar)
- Maternity Leave: 26 weeks (as per Maternity Benefit Act)
- Paternity Leave: 10 working days

Leave approval process:
1. Submit leave request via HR portal at least 3 working days in advance
2. Manager approval required for all leaves
3. Leaves during project deadlines require VP approval
4. Unapproved absence for 3+ days may trigger disciplinary review

=== Compensation & Benefits ===

Salary structure:
- Basic Pay: 40% of CTC
- HRA: 20% of CTC
- Special Allowance: 25% of CTC
- PF Contribution: 12% of Basic (employee + employer)
- Gratuity: As per Payment of Gratuity Act (after 5 years)

Benefits:
- Health Insurance: Group mediclaim for employee + spouse + 2 children (Sum insured: ₹5,00,000)
- Dental: Covered under group mediclaim
- Learning Budget: ₹50,000 per year for courses, conferences, certifications
- Home Office Stipend: ₹15,000 one-time setup allowance for new joiners
- Meal Allowance: ₹200/day on in-office days (via Sodexo card)

=== Performance Reviews ===

Review cycle: Bi-annual (April and October)
- Self-assessment submitted by employee
- 360-degree feedback from peers (minimum 3 reviewers)
- Manager assessment with goal tracking
- Calibration meeting with leadership team
- Compensation revision effective from April cycle only
- Promotion eligibility: Minimum 18 months in current role

Rating scale:
1 - Needs Improvement
2 - Meets Some Expectations
3 - Meets Expectations
4 - Exceeds Expectations
5 - Outstanding

=== Code of Conduct ===

All employees must:
- Maintain confidentiality of client data and proprietary algorithms
- Report any security incidents to security@grahvity.ai within 24 hours
- Complete mandatory compliance training quarterly
- Follow the Acceptable Use Policy for company devices and networks
- Declare any conflicts of interest annually

Zero tolerance policies:
- Harassment or discrimination of any kind
- Sharing trading algorithms or strategies with external parties
- Using company resources for personal trading
- Misrepresenting credentials or work output
